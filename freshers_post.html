Intro TO IITJ

A brief guide to IITJ Kind of life by:- Shivang Khandelwal


Institute life

One of the most exciting and precious era of a person's life. In here, a person learn everything which he cannot encounter at home. Unlike home, it's a place away from comfort zone. You need to do everything yourself until you meet someone who is willing to accompany. Institute life is totally incomplete without hostel life. You struggle in an institute for attendance and submissions but in hostel you relax yourself. You enjoy, you make memories, you do everything you felt like doing at your home but were unable to execute due to restrictions. So now, let's jump onto hostels, a place full of memories.

Diwali is the largest festival celebrated in the Nation. Here at IITJ, work starts early and the whole campus is decorated and the altar is made ready. The large rangolis, floral arches and more importantly the fun we have with our new family here is all a precious memory. This festival brings out the Home out of the campus.

LOHRI

It is a special celebration as it is completely organised by the freshmen. It is a new experience decorating, marketing, and arranging a complete celebration with no one but your peers and friends. The freshmen invite their seniors and teachers to the celebration and it is a proud moment to present the event to their superiors. Then celebrating around the large Bonfire with everyone is a memorable site.

NIMBLE

Nimble, the intra college technical fest provides a platform to the techno buds of the college to show their hidden talent. Nimble comprises of four action packed days filled with a great variety of tech and science events ranging from intense mind boggling events such as robotics, Electronics, programming to fun filled events like angry bird, quizzes, crypto, robot wars, fun games etc. Apart from the events, the talks by eminent personalities in the field of Science and Technology are organized to motivate students to work harder as there is no end to discoveries and inventions.
