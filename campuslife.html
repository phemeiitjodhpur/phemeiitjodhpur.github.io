FESTIVALS

Here at IITJ life’s cauldron always keeps boiling. With festivals, events, programs occurring almost all year round. Firstly about the Festivals we celebrate on Campus.

GANESH CHATURTHI

A grand celebration with a large Deity, Pooja, procession, and of course festive send off.

DIWALI

Diwali is the largest festival celebrated in the Nation. Here at IITJ, work starts early and the whole campus is decorated and the altar is made ready. The large rangolis, floral arches and more importantly the fun we have with our new family here is all a precious memory. The true beauty of the festival is when the whole campus be it hostels ,faculty quarters ,institute roads, lecture hall buildings ….just everything is lit to life by literally a 1000 or even more oil lamps the sight is just a heart-warming delight to watch and even satisfying to watch after that hard work. This festival brings out the Home out of the campus.

LOHRI

It is a special celebration as it is completely organised by the freshmen. It is a new experience decorating, marketing, and arranging a complete celebration with no one but your peers and friends. The freshmen invite their seniors and teachers to the celebration and it is a proud moment to present the event to their superiors. Then celebrating around the large Bonfire with everyone is a memorable site.

HOLI

The festival of colours is a grand and festive occasion at the campus where the whole student community come together on the grounds and colour each other…. These festivals bind us into a family.

GYMKHANA EVENTS

Apart from the traditional festivals there events hosted by the Gymkhana

SPANDAN

Month’s together of early lectures ,midterms, quizzes, does keep you busy and working then comes Spandan….three days pure joy and only fun. It is an intra-hostel tournament of basically who has most fun. Drama, art, design, dancing, singing, literature, photography events, informals, fashion show and a lot more coloured under a theme to feed your talents.

NIMBLE

Nimble, the intra college technical fest provides a platform to the techno buds of the college to show their hidden talent. Nimble comprises of four action packed days filled with a great variety of tech and science events ranging from intense mind boggling events such as robotics, Electronics, programming to fun filled events like angry bird, quizzes, crypto, robot wars, fun games etc. Apart from the events, the talks by eminent personalities in the field of Science and Technology are organized to motivate students to work harder as there is no end to discoveries and inventions.

FRAMED

It is an annual display of artwork by our students. Art of any manner, genre, size, or shape is displayed in a jaw-dropping gallery for visitors, students and faculty to feast their eyes on. Both students and teachers alike come up with astonishing creations and ornate canvases.

IGNUS

North-west India’s largest socio-cultural-techno fest, Ignus is an Inter-institute fest where students from all over the country come to indulge in magical grandeur IIT Jodhpur has to offer. The fest gives a platform to some of the country’s best hidden and unfathomed talents. In 2019 a staggering 2000 students walked into the blissful world of Myth and Magic. Cultural competitions, technical Workshops, Guest Lectures, DJ nights, fiery dance offs, pageants and lot more…..Well known stars like Mika Singh, Javed Ali, Shilpa Rao, The Local Train, Papon etc. have amazed the students with their performances .
